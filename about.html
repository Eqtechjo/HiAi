About Us

Navigating Innovations: HiAi began with a strong foundation in professional video surveillance products. By 2019, while still maintaining our initial offerings, we ventured into smart home security, steered by our vision-centered AIoT platform.

Catering to Distinct Demands: For our home users, we address the need for straightforward, user-friendly security solutions complemented by elements of social entertainment. For our small-to-medium business clientele, we prioritize simple yet cost-effective security monitoring solutions.

Commitment to Quality: Our relentless pursuit of quality has earned us esteemed certifications such as ISO9001, ISO14001, BSCI, CE, FCC, and RoHS. Powering this commitment is our robust team of over 300 professionals, ensuring we uphold the highest standards in everything we do.

Our Ethos: “Smart security, better life” encapsulates our mission. Our endeavor is to incorporate cutting-edge technology smoothly into everyday life, continually enhancing standards of security and ease.

Embark with HiAi, where innovation aligns seamlessly with today’s diverse requirements.
